Communicating with other members

There are 3 official Sandbox communication platforms:

  • The Facebook group
  • The Newsletter
  • The Slack

Once you become a member you will be added to all 3.

Goal of the facebook group

The Facebook group used to be the central hub to much of the community communication. It is however quite hard to structurally keep track of so much awesomeness on a platform like Facebook.

Goal of the newsletter

The newsletter keeps you up to date on what is happening in the community. We send around updates on what HQ has been working on and news from our members.

Goal of the Slack

Slack is a chat-style application where we can create groups, channels and person-to-person conversations.

Slack is used for member-to-member communication. In the slack you can talk to Sandbox hubs or to members indivudually.. Here is how it works:

  • As a member you will receive a personal invite to join the Slack
  • Once you finish your account you can log in through your browser
  • We recommend downloading the Slack desktop app ( or the all in one Franz app )
  • We recommend downloading the slack mobile app

The slack is populated with channels for each Hub.
